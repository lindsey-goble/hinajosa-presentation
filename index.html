Space Planning

Lindsey will reconfigure & finalize floor plans to include areas listed in scope of work. Floor plan will be reconfigured in a way that makes future addition feasible. Detailed construction documents will be delivered to client and contractor.

Design Development

Lindsey will meet with client to understand their design taste and create room-by-room concept boards with preliminary material selections and inspiration images. Lindsey will finalize selecting all materials with approval of client. Specification + Contractor Coordination

Lindsey will produce detailed specs, drawings & rendered images for contractors to clearly understand and execute; including dimensions, materials & finish schedules, fixture placements, etc. Lindsey will coordinate directly with contractors, vendors, and any other party involved to ensure the project is executed timely, efficiently and correctly.

05
Ongoing Support

Lindsey will be available throughout the project until completion for questions, concerns and resolutions, ensuring the end result meets the clients expectations & desires.
